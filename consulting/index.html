From xxx to xxx i was part of the SAGE team in the Hybrid Forecasting Challenge. The goal was to take Good Judgement-style forecasting questions with human forecasters, and see how to mash them up with model-based forecasts. I worked on the latter. Developed a microservice based on time-series models and with a web API (REST; Docker) that could semi-automatically provide model-based forecasts for natural language question like “How many protests will ACLED report in Benin between 7 August and 9 September YYYY?”.
